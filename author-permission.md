# Non-Exclusive Permission Template

Authors can copy/paste and sign the following statement when granting permission to host their paper:

> I, ______________________, certify that I am the author/co-author of the paper titled “______________________.” I grant the UNLV ECON 490 Journal a non-exclusive license to host, display, and distribute the paper in digital form. I retain all copyrights to the work and may publish it elsewhere. I affirm that the submission does not infringe on the rights of others and contains no confidential or sensitive personal information.
>
> Signature: ______________________  
> Date: ______________________

Send the signed statement to **econ-journal@example.edu** along with your PDF and metadata.
