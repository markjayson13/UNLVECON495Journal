# Ethics and academic integrity

- Submit only original work authored by the listed contributors.
- Cite all data, code, and sources used in the analysis.
- Plagiarism or falsification of results may result in removal from the proceedings.
- Disclose any substantial AI assistance (drafting, translation, coding, or analysis) in the methodology or acknowledgments. Authors remain responsible for all content.
